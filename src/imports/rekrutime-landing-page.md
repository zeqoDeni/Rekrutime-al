Create a high-fidelity, modern iOS-style landing page for an Albanian audience for an app called “Rekruti.me” (recruitment/time management platform). Use a clean, light, white-first interface with subtle grays and soft neutral accents. The design must be easy to scan and read, mobile-first, and feel like a premium iOS product.

Global style:
- Visual direction: iOS-inspired UI, generous whitespace, rounded cards (18–22px radius), thin borders, soft shadows, minimal gradients.
- Theme: Modern light theme only. Background should stay mostly white (#F7F7F8) with muted off-whites and light slate text contrast.
- Typography: Use a clean geometric/modern sans similar to SF Pro. Primary size hierarchy for readability:
  - H1: 44–52
  - H2: 32–38
  - Body: 16–18
  - Meta/captions: 13–14
- Colors:
  - Primary: #007AFF (Apple blue, used sparingly for actions)
  - Secondary/accent: #34C759 (subtle success), #FF9F0A (warning)
  - Text: #0F172A / #334155
  - Borders: #E5E7EB
  - Card background: #FFFFFF
- Layout: Max width 1120–1240px, 12-column grid, strong rhythm, sections separated by breathing space.
- UI components: iOS-like buttons with filled/outline variants, pill labels, segmented toggles, info chips, glassy stats cards.
- Interaction style: subtle fade/slide reveal animation on scroll; button hover lift + gentle shadow increase; micro-details but not flashy.

Page structure:
1) Navbar:
- Brand + short one-line value proposition.
- 4 top links: Features, Si funksionon, Çmimet, Kontakt.
- Primary CTA button: “Filloni falas”
- Secondary CTA: “Shkarko aplikacionin” (if applicable)
- Keep header sticky with soft blur.

2) Hero section:
- Headline in Albanian: “Gjeste menaxhimi pune më të thjeshta për ekipet e tua.”
- Subtext: “Planifikoni intervistat, kohët e punës dhe komunikimin me një panel të pastër dhe të shpejtë.”
- CTA pair: “Filloni tani” + “Shiko demonstrimin”.
- Right side: iPhone-style card mockup with dashboard preview (screenshot-like placeholder).
- Add trust indicators below CTAs (e.g. “Përdoret nga ekipe nga Tirana, Durrësi, Prishtina” and simple numeric metrics).

3) Problem/Solution section:
- 3 cards with icon + title + short text in Albanian.
- Emphasize clarity, speed, and coordination.

4) “How it works” section:
- 3-step horizontal flow (desktop) / vertical accordion cards (mobile):
  1. Krijo llogarinë
  2. Planifiko rrjedhat
  3. Ndaje rolin me ekipin

5) Features section:
- 2-row grid of 6 cards:
  - Planifikimi
  - Njoftime WhatsApp
  - Kontroll i pranisë
  - Dashboard i thjeshtë
  - Mbështetje lokale
  - Eksport i shpejtë raporti
- Include short Albanian descriptions, one icon each.

6) Social proof:
- 3 short testimonials in Albanian with Albanian names/locations.
- Add review style chips and star rating.

7) Pricing:
- 3 cards: Free, Pro, Biznesi.
- Highlight “Pro” with badge and recommended style.
- Clear monthly price and main feature bullets.

8) FAQ:
- 4 accordion items in Albanian:
  - A duhet aplikacion i veçantë?
  - A mbështeten numra në gjuhën shqipe?
  - A mund ta përdorë ekipi im?
  - Si funksionon mbrojtja e të dhënave?

9) Final CTA:
- Strong closing section with white background and large contrast.
- Headline: “Bëni hapin tjetër më inteligjent.”
- CTA button primary.

10) Footer:
- 4 link columns, social icons, legal, contact info.
- Keep minimal and clean.

Visual assets:
- Include placeholders for: Albanian people, Albanian office/team scenes, calendar/interview screens, phone mockups.
- Use warm natural photos with professional tone.
- Maintain a fresh, hopeful, trustworthy mood.

Output requirements:
- Create both desktop (1440) and mobile (390) artboards.
- Keep margins comfortable for iOS touch targets (min button height 44px).
- Provide components + styles for easy reuse.
- All text in Albanian, culturally clear and locally understandable.
- Ensure final design passes readability checks (WCAG-like contrast and high legibility on small screens).
